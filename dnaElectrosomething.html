Introduction

Is race biologically real? In our experiment, we extract DNA to see if we match with people of the same race or dont.

The Process

First off, we began by extracting DNA from the inside of our cheeks and put them in a container, along with liquid buffer and it was boild. After boiling, we added stainer to make it more visible, and we put our DNA in the gel using micro-pipeters, and use magnets to pull the DNA through the gel. This allowed us to see how long our DNA strands were, and we compared it to classmate. In the end we machted only with one person within our race, and 4 people outside of it.
