Introduction

There are many ways to convince people of something or to influence them, one of those ways being artwork. In The Screen printing project, our goal was to convice, help, inspire, or remind people of something important. For mine, I chose to make a screen print about procrastonation, mainly because I struggle with doing homework till the last day or moments its due, which really isn't great, and is a bad habit many must break free from. My art was meant to remind people to keep going forward, and not to wait for anything, and going to guide you through the process of The Screen Printing Project

The Process

First we began brain storming on how we would depict our messages in the form of art. I chose to make someone climbing a mountain or cliff edge whilst carrying a flag. At the top of the mountain there was on ovbious spot for the flag. Flag representing goal, you climbing is you mocving forward and working hard, and flag placement area represent the end of the goal, as like conquering it. After generating ideas, I had someone take a picture of me from a side view, while i was in a "climbing position", and imported that image into photoshop. I made myself one solid color, and added it to the mountain, as well as the flag on my back. After our entire design was complete, we worked on frame dimensions, which was dependent on the size of our design. This step allowed to get into actually building the frames. We used wood, and cut each side at a 45* angle, allowing us to put each together nicely with glue, and holding it tight with staples. After glue is done drying, we strech a film over the wood extremely tight, and stapled it to the back of the frame. We then put our designs into an App to pring them, pick out the designs, and iron them on. The the last step was putting the frame over something and putting paint through it, putting our designs on whatever we want, however many times we'd like.
